Interests and Hobbies

The Great Outdoors

I am a big nature person, in every season youll find me somewhere other than home spending time in the outdoors, whether it's hiking in the mountains, snowboarding the slopes, picking fresh strawberries and flowers, or swimming at the beach. I have always loved spending time outside, and I will continue to incorporate that into my daily life. I believe that its important for one's happines and health to do so. It is my dream to travel all overe the world and be able to experience the culture and environment of so many unique places.



Health

I take great pride in my health. I have spent a lot of time researching and studying nutrition, ayurveda, and overall health. I originally went to school for neuroscience, and then transitioned to ayurveda and holistic medicine. I decided to take my studies and incorporate them into my lifestyle rather than working within the field. I workout every day for two hours, either at the gym or running in the park. I make sure that I am eating the right diet, currated for me and my personal health.
